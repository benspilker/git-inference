Bemenderfer LLC (also styled as Bemenderfer) is a small, privately held software consulting firm based in Indianapolis, Indiana.⁠LinkedIn

Key Details
Founded: 2008 (about 18 years in operation as of 2026) by David Bemenderfer, who serves as Founder and President.⁠Rocketreach
Size: 2–10 employees (reports vary between ~5).⁠LinkedIn
Focus: Specialized Epicor ERP (including Kinetic, Vantage, and Vista) consulting, implementation, training, streamlining, integration, and optimization. They also provide general business process analysis, improvement, and custom software solutions.⁠Bemenderfer
Proprietary Product: DataPortal — a mobile-friendly tool for giving field/mobile teams real-time access to ERP data and functions.⁠Bemenderfer
Services

They help manufacturers and other businesses maximize their Epicor ERP investment through:

ERP consulting and implementation
Process optimization and gap analysis
Training and troubleshooting
Integrations (e.g., CAD/BOM, web systems, SQL reporting)
Custom automation and reporting (Crystal Reports, SSRS, Method/ Data Directives)

They serve clients across industries including aerospace, food production, industrial manufacturing, and automotive.⁠Bemenderfer
