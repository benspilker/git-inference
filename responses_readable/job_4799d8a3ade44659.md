Making a pizza can be broken down into several simple steps. Here's a basic process:

1. Prepare the Dough

Ingredients:

2 ½ cups of all-purpose flour

1 tsp of salt

1 tsp of sugar

1 packet of active dry yeast (about 2 ¼ tsp)

1 cup warm water (about 110°F)

1 tbsp of olive oil

Instructions:

Dissolve the sugar in warm water, then sprinkle the yeast over it. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until frothy.

In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.

Stir the ingredients together until a dough forms, then knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth.

Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.

2. Prepare the Sauce

Ingredients:

1 can (15 oz) of crushed tomatoes

1-2 cloves garlic (minced)

1 tsp of olive oil

Salt, pepper, and dried oregano to taste

Instructions:

Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for about 1 minute.

Add crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Prepare the Toppings

Ingredients:

Shredded mozzarella cheese (about 2 cups)

Any additional toppings you like (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)

Instructions:

Slice and chop any vegetables or meats you want to add.

4. Assemble the Pizza

Preheat your oven to about 475°F (245°C).

Punch down the risen dough and divide it into individual portions (for personal pizzas) or roll it into one large pizza.

Roll the dough out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

Transfer the rolled dough onto a pizza stone or baking sheet.

Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

Add your desired toppings.

5. Bake the Pizza

Place the pizza in the preheated oven on the middle rack for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

6. Serve

Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice and enjoy!

This process can be adapted by experimenting with different toppings, sauces, and even types of cheese.
