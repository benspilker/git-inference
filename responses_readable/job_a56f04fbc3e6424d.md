Making pizza at home is super fun and customizable! Here’s a simple guide to get you started with making a classic pizza:

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast (or one packet)

1 ½ cups warm water (about 110°F)

1 tsp sugar

3 ½ cups all-purpose flour

1 ½ tsp salt

1 tbsp olive oil (plus extra for greasing)

For the Sauce:

1 can (15 oz) crushed tomatoes

1 tbsp olive oil

1 garlic clove (minced)

1 tsp dried oregano

½ tsp salt

½ tsp sugar

Fresh basil (optional)

Toppings:

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Other toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Dough:

In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5–10 minutes until the yeast becomes frothy.

Add the flour, salt, and olive oil into the yeast mixture. Stir until it forms a dough.

Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5–7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

Grease a bowl with olive oil, place the dough inside, and cover with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise for about 1–1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.

2. Make the Sauce:

In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and sugar. Stir to combine and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes to thicken.

Optionally, you can add fresh basil for extra flavor.

3. Assemble the Pizza:

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two portions if you want two pizzas or keep it whole for one large pizza.

Roll out the dough on a floured surface until it’s about 12 inches in diameter (for a large pizza).

Place the dough on a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

Add your preferred toppings.

4. Bake:

Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes before slicing.

5. Enjoy!

Slice it up and enjoy your homemade pizza!
