Easiest customer types

The best customers are people who already know they need marketing or admin help but do not want to hire an employee. That usually means local businesses, solo professionals, coaches, real estate agents, dentists, gyms, restaurants, and small e-commerce shops. They are easier to reach because they already have obvious pain points and usually respond to simple, direct offers.
