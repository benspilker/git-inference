The Renaissance period is generally considered to have started in Italy in the late 14th century, around 1370–1400, and it spread across Europe over the following centuries. It is commonly regarded as ending in the early 17th century, roughly around 1600–1620, although the exact dates vary depending on the region and the cultural or artistic context.
