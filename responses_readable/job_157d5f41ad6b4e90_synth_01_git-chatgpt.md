Bemenderfer LLC is a small, privately held consulting firm based in Indianapolis, Indiana, founded in 2008 by David Bemenderfer. The company specializes in Epicor ERP solutions, particularly for Epicor Kinetic, Vantage, and Vista platforms. Its services include ERP consulting, implementation, system optimization, integration, and training. Additionally, they offer custom solutions such as a proprietary tool called DataPortal, which provides mobile access to ERP data.

Bemenderfer LLC serves industries like aerospace, automotive, industrial manufacturing, and food production. The company is noted for its boutique approach, with a team of 2-10 employees. Key offerings include ERP deployment, process streamlining, gap analysis, custom reporting (using tools like Crystal Reports and SSRS), and integration of various business systems.

If you are looking into their services or considering them for an ERP project, they emphasize end-to-end consulting and custom solutions, making them a specialized choice for businesses looking to maximize their Epicor ERP systems.
