Making a pizza is simple! Here's a basic recipe for a delicious homemade pizza:

Ingredients:

Dough:

2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 tbsp olive oil

1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast

1 cup warm water (about 110°F or 43°C)

Tomato Sauce:

1 cup crushed tomatoes

1 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp garlic powder

Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings:

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Your choice of toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.

Instructions:

Prepare the dough:

In a large bowl, mix the flour, salt, and sugar.

In a separate bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the dry ingredients, and mix to form a dough.

Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.

Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour (or until it doubles in size).

Prepare the sauce:

In a small saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.

Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and let simmer for 10-15 minutes to let the flavors meld. Remove from heat.

Assemble the pizza:

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 2 portions (for 2 medium pizzas). Roll each portion into a circle or your desired shape on a floured surface.

Place the rolled-out dough onto a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce and add your favorite toppings.

Bake:

Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

Remove from the oven, let cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy!
