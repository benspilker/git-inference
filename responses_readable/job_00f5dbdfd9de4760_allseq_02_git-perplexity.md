Here’s a simple homemade pizza method.

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet active dry yeast

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

For the sauce:

1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes

1 minced garlic clove

1 tsp dried oregano

1/2 tsp salt

Optional: 1/2 tsp sugar

Toppings:

Mozzarella cheese

Pepperoni, vegetables, mushrooms, onions, or anything you like

Dough

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Let it sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

In a larger bowl, mix flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.

Stir until a dough forms, then knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth.

Put the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise 1–2 hours until doubled.

Sauce

Mix tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar.

Simmer for 10–15 minutes, then let it cool.

Assemble

Heat your oven to 475 F.

Roll out the dough on a floured surface.

Put it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread on sauce, add cheese, then toppings.

Bake

Bake for 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

Faster option

A quick no-rise pizza dough also works well: mix yeast, warm water, flour, salt, and oil, then bake right away for about 15–20 minutes at 450 F to 475 F.
