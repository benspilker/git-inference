Simple Homemade Pizza (makes one 12-inch pizza)

Ingredients (dough):
2 cups (250g) all-purpose or bread flour
¾ cup (180ml) warm water (105–110°F / 40–43°C)
1 tsp instant yeast
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp olive oil
Ingredients (toppings):
½ cup pizza sauce or marinara
1½–2 cups shredded mozzarella
Toppings of choice (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)
Olive oil, oregano, red pepper flakes (optional)
Steps:
Make dough: Mix warm water + yeast. Add flour, salt, oil. Stir until shaggy, then knead 5–7 minutes until smooth. (Or use store-bought dough to skip this.)
Rise: Cover and let rest 30–60 minutes (or up to 2 hours) until doubled. Preheat oven to 475–500°F (245–260°C) with pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
Shape: Punch down dough, roll/stretch into 12-inch circle on floured surface or parchment.
Assemble: Brush crust lightly with oil. Spread sauce (leave ½-inch border), add cheese and toppings sparingly.
Bake: Slide onto hot stone/sheet. Bake 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
Finish: Let cool 2 minutes, slice, and serve.

Tips: Don’t overload toppings. For crispier crust, use a pizza stone and high heat. Total time: ~1–1.5 hours (mostly hands-off).

Enjoy! Adjust toppings to your taste.
