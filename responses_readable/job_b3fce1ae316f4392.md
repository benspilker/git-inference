Making pizza at home is fun and rewarding! Here’s a simple way to do it, from making the dough to baking your pizza:

Ingredients:
For the dough:

2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (one packet)

1 ½ cups warm water (110°F/45°C)

3 ½ cups all-purpose flour

2 teaspoons salt

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 teaspoon sugar

For the sauce:

1 cup tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)

1 tablespoon olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings:

1-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, or any toppings you like

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Dough:

Activate yeast: In a small bowl, mix the warm water with sugar and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes foamy.

Mix the dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center, then pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until the dough starts to come together.

Knead the dough: On a floured surface, knead the dough for about 5-8 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.

Let it rise: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a clean cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

2. Prepare the Sauce:

In a small bowl, combine the tomato sauce, olive oil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well. You can also cook the sauce for 10-15 minutes on low heat for a more robust flavor, but it’s optional.

3. Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it’s preheating.

4. Shape the Dough:

Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it in half (for two pizzas).

Roll out one portion of dough on a floured surface into your desired shape (round or rectangular). If you’re using a pizza stone, transfer the rolled dough to a piece of parchment paper for easy sliding into the oven.

5. Assemble the Pizza:

Spread the pizza sauce over the rolled dough.

Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

Add your desired toppings.

6. Bake the Pizza:

Carefully slide the pizza (on the parchment paper) onto the preheated stone or a baking sheet.

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

7. Enjoy!:

Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes.

Slice and serve!

You can get creative with the toppings and make it your own! Have you thought about what kind of toppings you want?
