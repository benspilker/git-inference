Here’s a simple homemade pizza method:

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 packet active dry yeast

1 tsp sugar

1 tsp salt

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

1 cup pizza sauce

1 to 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Toppings you like: pepperoni, vegetables, olives, mushrooms, etc.

Dough

Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit 5 to 10 minutes until foamy.

In a bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.

Stir until a dough forms, then knead for 8 to 10 minutes until smooth.

Cover and let rise for 1 to 2 hours, until doubled.

Assemble

Preheat your oven to 475 F.

Roll out the dough on a floured surface.

Move it to a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread sauce over the dough, leaving a border.

Add cheese and toppings.

Bake

Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Let it cool a few minutes before slicing.

Quick tip

Use less topping than you think you need so the pizza stays crisp.
