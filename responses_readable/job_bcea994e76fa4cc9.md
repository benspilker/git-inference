Here's a basic pizza recipe for you!

Ingredients:

Pizza Dough (store-bought or homemade)

2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 tbsp sugar

1 tbsp active dry yeast

1 cup warm water

1 tbsp olive oil

Pizza Sauce:

1 cup canned crushed tomatoes

1 clove garlic (minced)

1 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp dried basil

Salt & pepper to taste

Toppings:

1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers, or any other toppings you like

Instructions:
1. Prepare the Dough:

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.

Add the flour, salt, and olive oil, then stir until it forms a dough.

Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Let the dough rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

2. Make the Sauce:

Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic for 1 minute.

Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.

Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

3. Assemble the Pizza:

Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Roll the dough out to your desired thickness on a floured surface.

Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough.

Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your preferred toppings.

4. Bake the Pizza:

Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

5. Serve:

Slice and enjoy! You can garnish with fresh basil or a drizzle of olive oil if desired.
