Basic Homemade Pizza Guide
Dough (makes 2 medium pizzas):
500g bread flour (or all-purpose)
325ml warm water (~110°F/43°C)
7g instant yeast (1 packet)
10g salt
15ml olive oil
Optional: 5g sugar for faster rise
Mix flour, yeast, salt (and sugar if using). Add water and oil.
Knead 8–10 min until smooth and elastic.
Let rise covered at room temp 1–2 hours (or refrigerate 24–72h for better flavor).
Simple Sauce:
400g canned crushed tomatoes
2 cloves garlic, minced
15ml olive oil, salt, oregano, black pepper to taste
Simmer 10 min or use raw for a fresher taste.
Assembly & Baking:
Preheat oven to highest setting (500°F/260°C+). Use a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet if available.
Divide dough, stretch (don't roll) into 12-inch rounds.
Lightly sauce, add cheese (low-moisture mozzarella works best), then toppings.
Bake 8–12 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.
Rest 2–3 min before slicing.
Pro tips:
Less sauce/cheese = crispier crust.
Pre-cook wet veggies (mushrooms, spinach) to avoid sogginess.
For Neapolitan-style: use "00" flour and bake at 700°F+ if your oven allows.
