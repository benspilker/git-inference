Here’s a simple homemade pizza guide.

Ingredients

For 1 medium pizza:

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet active dry yeast

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

For sauce:

1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes

1 clove garlic, minced

1 tsp oregano

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 tsp sugar, optional

Toppings:

1 to 2 cups shredded mozzarella

Pepperoni, vegetables, olives, mushrooms, or anything you like

Dough

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Let it sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

In a larger bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.

Stir until a dough forms, then knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth.

Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise 1–2 hours until doubled in size.

Sauce

Combine tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar in a small pan.

Simmer 10–15 minutes on low heat.

Set aside.

Assemble and bake

Heat oven to 475°F.

Roll out the dough on a floured surface.

Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread sauce over the dough.

Add cheese and toppings.

Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Tips

Preheat the oven fully for a better crust.

Use less topping than you think; too much makes it soggy.

If you want faster pizza, a no-rise dough works well too.
