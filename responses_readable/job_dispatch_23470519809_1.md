I don’t have the ability to see your IP address or your geographic location. I can, however, guide you on how to find them yourself:

To find your public IPv4 address:

Open a web browser and visit a site like https://whatismyipaddress.com
 or https://ipinfo.io
.

It will show your public IPv4 address and usually an approximate location (city/region and country).

To find your local/private IPv4 address (on your device):

Windows: Open Command Prompt and type ipconfig. Look for “IPv4 Address.”

Mac/Linux: Open Terminal and type ifconfig or ip addr.
