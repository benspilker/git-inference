To make pizza dough, combine 500 g (about 4 cups) of bread‑flour, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp sugar in a bowl; dissolve 2 tsp active dry yeast in 300 ml warm water (≈110 °F/43 °C) and let it bloom for 5 minutes, then stir the yeast mixture into the dry ingredients, add 2 tbsp olive oil, and knead until smooth and elastic (about 8‑10 minutes), cover and let the dough rise in a warm spot for 1‑1½ hours until doubled, punch it down, shape into a ball, and let it rest 10‑15 minutes before stretching or rolling into a pizza crust.
