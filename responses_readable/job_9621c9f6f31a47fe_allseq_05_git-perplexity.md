Easy starter pizza

2 cups flour.

1 packet yeast.

1 tsp salt.

3/4 cup warm water, roughly.

1 tbsp olive oil.

Tomato sauce.

Mozzarella.

Optional toppings like pepperoni, onion, basil, or peppers.

Baking tips

Preheat the oven well, and if you have one, preheat a baking tray or pizza stone so the bottom cooks faster.
