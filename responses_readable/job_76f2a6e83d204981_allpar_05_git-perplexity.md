Here’s a simple homemade pizza method.

Ingredients

For the dough:

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.

1 packet active dry yeast, about 2 1/4 tsp.

1 tsp salt.

1 tsp sugar.

1 cup warm water.

2 tbsp olive oil.

For the sauce:

1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes.

1 clove garlic, minced.

1 tsp dried oregano.

1/2 tsp salt.

1/2 tsp sugar, optional.

For toppings:

Shredded mozzarella.

Pepperoni, vegetables, olives, mushrooms, or anything you like.

Steps

Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

In a large bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.

Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Put the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 1–2 hours until doubled.

Make the sauce by simmering tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar for 10–15 minutes.

Preheat the oven to 475°F.

Roll out the dough, place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone, add sauce, cheese, and toppings.

Bake for 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Helpful tips

Preheat the oven fully for a better crust.

Use less sauce and fewer toppings to avoid a soggy pizza.

A pizza stone or hot baking sheet helps the bottom crisp up.
