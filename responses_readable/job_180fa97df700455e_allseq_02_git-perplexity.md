Here’s a simple homemade pizza method:

What you need

2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

1 packet active dry yeast

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 cup tomato sauce

1–2 cups shredded mozzarella

Your favorite toppings

Dough

Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

In a bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, then mix into a dough.

Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth.

Cover and let it rise for 1–2 hours, until doubled.

Sauce

Mix tomato sauce, minced garlic if you have it, oregano, salt, and a little sugar.

Simmer for 10–15 minutes, then cool.

Assemble

Heat the oven to 475°F.

Roll out the dough on a floured surface.

Put it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread sauce, add cheese, then toppings.

Bake

Bake for 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
