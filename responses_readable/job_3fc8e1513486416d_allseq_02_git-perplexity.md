Here’s a simple homemade pizza you can make tonight.

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.

1 packet active dry yeast.

1 tsp sugar.

1 tsp salt.

1 cup warm water.

2 tbsp olive oil.

1 cup pizza sauce or crushed tomatoes.

1 to 2 cups shredded mozzarella.

Your favorite toppings.

Dough

Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit 5 to 10 minutes until foamy.

In a bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.

Knead for 8 to 10 minutes until smooth.

Put the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 1 to 2 hours, until doubled.

Sauce

Mix tomato sauce, a little garlic, oregano, and salt.

Simmer for about 10 minutes if you want it thicker and richer.

Assemble

Heat your oven to 475°F.

Roll or stretch the dough into a circle on a floured surface.

Put it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread sauce over it, add cheese, then toppings.

Bake

Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Let it rest for a couple minutes, then slice.

Easy tip

If you want a faster version, use instant yeast or even pre-made dough. That cuts the wait a lot.
