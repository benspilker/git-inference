General tactics to win high‑paying gigs

Polished profile & niche tagline

Use a headline that combines skill + result (e.g., “React Developer – Launch MVPs in 7 Days”).
Add a professional photo and a short “About” that mentions years of experience, tools, and success metrics.

Portfolio with measurable outcomes

Show before/after, screenshots, or screenshots of analytics.
Include a brief bullet list of results (traffic ↑, conversion ↑, time saved).

Pricing strategy

Start with a mid‑range price to attract first reviews, then raise rates after 5‑10 positive reviews.
Offer tiered packages (Basic/Standard/Premium) to capture low‑budget and high‑budget clients.

Client communication

Respond within 1–2 hours.
Use a structured proposal: scope, timeline, deliverables, revisions, and payment milestones.

Upsell & repeat business

After delivering the first project, suggest a maintenance retainer, monthly reporting, or additional features.
Offer a discount for a long‑term contract (e.g., 10 % off for a 3‑month commitment).

Leverage platform tools

On Upwork, use Project Catalog to create pre‑priced “fixed‑price” services.
On Fiverr, enable Gig Extras (fast delivery, extra revisions, source files).

Stay current

Follow trends (e.g., no‑code tools, AI‑generated content) and add them to your skill set.
Periodically refresh your portfolio with recent work.
