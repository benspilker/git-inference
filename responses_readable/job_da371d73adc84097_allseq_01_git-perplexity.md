Here’s a simple homemade pizza recipe.

Dough

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.

1 tsp salt.

1 tsp sugar.

1 packet active dry yeast.

1 cup warm water.

2 tbsp olive oil.

Sauce

1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes.

1 clove garlic, minced.

1 tsp dried oregano.

1/2 tsp salt.

1/2 tsp sugar, optional.

Toppings

1 to 2 cups shredded mozzarella.

Pepperoni, vegetables, olives, mushrooms, or whatever you like.

Steps

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit 5 to 10 minutes until foamy.

In a large bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.

Knead for 8 to 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Put the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 1 to 2 hours, until doubled.

Mix the sauce ingredients together.

Heat oven to 475°F.

Roll out the dough, spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.

Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Helpful tips

Preheat the oven fully for a better crust.

Use a pizza stone if you have one.

Don’t overload the pizza with toppings.

A no-rise pizza dough can also be made in about 20 minutes using a quick recipe with yeast, flour, water, oil, salt, and sugar, then baked right away.
