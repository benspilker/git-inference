Here's a concise step-by-step guide on how to make pizza at home:

Dough (makes 2–3 pizzas):

Ingredients: 500g flour (bread or all-purpose), 300–350ml warm water, 2 tsp instant yeast, 2 tsp salt, 2 tbsp olive oil.

Method:

Mix dry ingredients (flour, yeast, salt).

Add water and olive oil, stir until shaggy dough forms.

Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Cover and let rise 1-2 hours at room temperature, or refrigerate overnight for better flavor.

Sauce:

Ingredients: 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes, 1–2 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp dried oregano, pinch of sugar (optional), salt, pepper.

Method: Mix all ingredients cold; no cooking required.

Assembling:

Preheat oven to its highest setting (475–550°F / 245–290°C). Use a pizza stone or baking sheet.

Punch down dough, divide into balls, and let rest 15-20 minutes.

Stretch dough into thin rounds (avoid rolling pin for a fluffier crust).

Spread a thin layer of sauce, add desired toppings (cheese, veggies, meats).

Baking:

Bake for 7–10 minutes until crust is golden and toppings are bubbly.

Optional: for a crispier crust, use bread flour and let the dough ferment in the fridge for 24–48 hours.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
