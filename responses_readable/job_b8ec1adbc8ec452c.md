Absolutely! Let’s break out of the cereal rut with some quick, simple, and tasty breakfast ideas. I’ll give options ranging from super fast to a little more involved:

1. Eggs (super versatile)

Scrambled eggs – cook in a pan with a little butter or oil; add cheese, herbs, or veggies for variety.

Omelette – fold in veggies, cheese, or leftover meats.

Fried egg sandwich – fried egg + bread + avocado or tomato.

2. Toast variations

Avocado toast – smash avocado, sprinkle salt, pepper, maybe a fried egg on top.

Peanut butter banana toast – PB + banana slices + drizzle of honey.

Savory toast – cream cheese + smoked salmon + capers.

3. Quick warm options

Oatmeal – microwave oats with milk or water, then top with fruit, nuts, or cinnamon.

Yogurt parfait – layer yogurt, granola, and berries.

Breakfast quesadilla – tortilla + cheese + scrambled eggs; grill until golden.

4. Smoothie ideas

Green smoothie – spinach + banana + yogurt + milk.

Berry protein smoothie – frozen berries + milk + protein powder + a spoon of nut butter.

5. Leftover hacks

Breakfast burrito – leftover rice, beans, eggs, cheese in a tortilla.

Mini frittatas – bake eggs + veggies + cheese in muffin tins.
