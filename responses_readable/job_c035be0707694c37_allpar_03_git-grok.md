Bemenderfer LLC (also styled as Bemenderfer) is a small, privately held software consulting firm based in Indianapolis, Indiana, founded in 2008 by David Bemenderfer (Founder and President).⁠LinkedIn

Core Focus

The company specializes in Epicor Kinetic (and earlier Epicor ERP versions like Vantage and Vista). Services include:

Consulting
Implementation
Training
Streamlining
Integration
Customization
Business process optimization
Gap analysis
Reporting (Crystal Reports, SSRS)
Automation (Method Directives, Data Directives)

They also offer their own proprietary product: DataPortal, a mobile-friendly tool that provides real-time data and access for field/mobile workforces.⁠Bemenderfer

Additional Details
Company size: Approximately 2–10 employees (often listed as ~5).
Industries served: Aerospace, food production, industrial manufacturing, automotive, and others.
BBB status: Accredited since March 2025 (business started/incorporated August 2008).
Website: bemenderfer.com — emphasizes long-term client relationships, ERP optimization, and practical implementation support.⁠Bemenderfer

Note: There is a separate individual named Neil (T. Neil) Bemenderfer who is a civil mediator in Indianapolis with The Mediation Group LLC — unrelated to this company.
