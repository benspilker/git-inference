Here’s a straightforward, practical recipe for a classic homemade pizza:

Ingredients (makes 1 medium pizza)

Dough

2 ½ cups all‑purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active‑dry yeast
1 cup warm water (≈110 °F / 43 °C)
2 tbsp olive oil

Sauce

1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
½ tsp salt
½ tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)

Toppings (adjust to taste)

1–2 cups shredded mozzarella
Pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, olives, bell peppers, etc.
Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
Steps

Activate the yeast

In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in the warm water.
Let sit 5–10 min until foamy.

Mix the dough

In a large bowl combine flour and salt.
Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
Knead on a lightly floured surface 8–10 min, until smooth and elastic.
Lightly oil a clean bowl, place the dough inside, cover with a towel, and let rise 1–2 hours (or until doubled).

Prepare the sauce

In a saucepan combine tomato sauce, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and optional sugar.
Simmer on low 10–15 min, stirring occasionally. Set aside.

Pre‑heat the oven

Set to 475 °F (245 °C).
If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it heats.

Shape the crust

Punch down the risen dough.
Roll or stretch it to your desired thickness (≈¼‑inch) on a lightly floured surface.
Transfer to a parchment‑lined baking sheet or a pre‑heated pizza stone.

Assemble

Spread the sauce evenly, leaving a small border.
Sprinkle mozzarella, then add your chosen toppings.
Drizzle a little olive oil on top, if desired.

Bake

Slide the pizza into the oven.
Bake 12–15 min, until the crust is golden and the cheese bubbles.
For extra browning, broil 1–2 min at the end (watch closely).

Finish & serve

Remove, let rest 2–3 min.
Garnish with fresh basil, slice, and enjoy!

Quick tip: For a crispier bottom, use a pre‑heated pizza stone or an inverted baking sheet. If you’re short on time, you can skip the long rise by using “quick dough” (mix, let rest 15 min, then bake).

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
