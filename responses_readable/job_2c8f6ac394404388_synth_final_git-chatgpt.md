Here’s a concise, step-by-step guide to making pizza at home:

Prepare the Dough:

Mix 500 g bread flour (or Tipo 00/all-purpose), 2–2.5 tsp yeast, 1–2 tsp salt.

Add 300–338 ml lukewarm water and 1 tbsp olive oil; stir until shaggy.

Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Let it rise in a lightly oiled bowl, covered, for 1–2 hours until doubled.

Punch down, divide into balls, and rest 15–20 minutes before shaping.

Make the Sauce:

Combine 1 can (≈400 g) crushed tomatoes, 1–2 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp salt, and a pinch of sugar.

Optionally, add olive oil, balsamic vinegar, or fresh basil for extra flavor.

Assemble & Bake:

Preheat your oven as hot as possible (≈475–500°F / 245–260°C); use a pizza stone or heavy tray if available.

Stretch dough into rounds, spread a thin layer of sauce, and add cheese and toppings.

Bake for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Optional: Refrigerate dough for 24–48 hours to enhance flavor and achieve a crisper crust. Enjoy your homemade pizza!
