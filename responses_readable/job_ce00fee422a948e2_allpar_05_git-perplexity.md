Making pizza at home is straightforward with basic ingredients and a few steps. Here's a simple guide for one 12-inch pizza.

Ingredients

Dough (or use store-bought): 2.5 cups flour, 1 cup warm water, 1 packet yeast, 1 tsp sugar, 2 Tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp salt.
Sauce: 1/2 cup tomato sauce, 1 tsp oregano, garlic powder.
Toppings: 1-2 cups mozzarella cheese, pepperoni/sausage/veggies, olive oil.
sallysbakingaddiction

Dough Prep

Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water; let sit 5 minutes until foamy. Add flour, oil, salt; knead 5-7 minutes into a smooth ball. Cover and let rise 1 hour until doubled.

Sauce & Shaping

Stir sauce ingredients together. Punch down dough, roll into 12-inch circle on floured surface. Transfer to parchment-lined baking sheet or pizza stone.

Assemble & Bake

Preheat oven to 475-500°F. Spread sauce, add cheese and toppings; drizzle oil. Bake 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles. Cool slightly before slicing.
