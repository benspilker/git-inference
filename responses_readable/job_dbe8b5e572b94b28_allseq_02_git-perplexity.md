Here’s a simple home pizza recipe:

Preheat your oven to 475°F and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside if you have one.

Roll or stretch 1 ball of pizza dough into a thin round.

Put the dough on parchment paper or a lightly floured pan.

Spread on pizza sauce, then add shredded mozzarella and your toppings.

Bake for 10–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Let it cool for 2 minutes, slice, and serve.

Easy dough idea: use flour, warm water, yeast, salt, and a little olive oil. For a faster version, buy store-bought dough and just focus on toppings and baking.
