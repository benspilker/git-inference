How to accomplish them (step-by-step)

AI-assisted design and content services

What to offer

AI-generated copywriting packages (SEO blog posts, product descriptions, landing pages) plus human edits

Brand kits, social media templates, and editable design assets using AI tools

Short-form video scripts and storyboards with final polishing

How to win jobs

Build showpiece gigs with samples: high-conversion landing pages, 5–10 social posts, a 60-second promo script

Include a clear deliverable structure, revision limits, and delivery timelines

Emphasize speed and quality control: “AI-assisted, human-checked, 48-hour delivery”

Tips

Use AI tools to draft, then provide human-style edits for nuance and brand voice

Add case studies or before/after examples

Software and web development

What to offer

WordPress/WooCommerce site builds, plugin customization, performance optimization

Small SaaS MVPs, API integrations, automation scripts (Zapier/Make equivalents)

Bug fixes, security hardening, and code audits

How to win jobs

Create portfolio projects with live demos and performance metrics

Offer packaged pricing (starter, standard, pro) with defined scope

Prepare templates for requirements gathering and a technical proposal

Tips

Highlight measurable outcomes (load times, conversions, uptime)

Show familiarity with popular stacks (React/Vue, Node.js, Python)

Data and analytics

What to offer

Data cleaning, ETL pipelines, dashboards (Power BI/Tableau/Looker), SQL reporting

Automated reporting scripts and recurring data refresh setups

How to win jobs

Provide a sample dashboard with actionable metrics for a hypothetical client

Offer a trial 1–2 day data-cleaning mini-project at a reduced rate to win trust

Tips

Propose clear KPIs (e.g., churn reduction, revenue lift, operational efficiency)

Digital marketing and growth

What to offer

Paid media strategy (Google Ads, Meta) with funnel optimization

SEO audits and on-page optimization, content strategy, landing page CRO

Social media management with performance reporting

How to win jobs

Show ROI-focused case studies, even if sample data is synthetic

Offer phased engagements: audit, plan, execute, report

Tips

Provide clear attribution models and expected lift ranges

Cybersecurity and secure development

What to offer

Security assessments, threat modeling, secure coding reviews, compliance mapping

Incident response playbooks and vulnerability remediation guidance

How to win jobs

Emphasize certifications, methodologies (OWASP, NIST), and practical remediation plans

Provide a starter security audit template as a sample

Tips

Target small to mid-size businesses needing affordable, practical security help

Explainer videos, product demos, YouTube/video ads with motion graphics

Subtitles, captioning, and localization

Specialized consulting

What to offer

Niche advisory (e.g., fintech product strategy, compliance mapping, regulatory reporting)

How to win jobs

Target agencies and startups; show framework templates and a sample compliance checklist

Price by deliverable (audit, roadmap, implementation plan)

Tips

Build credibility with concise frameworks and practical outputs

Profile and proposal optimization tips

Niche down to a focused domain you can credibly own (e.g., “WordPress optimization for e-commerce”) rather than broad freelancing.

Create a strong portfolio: 3–5 long-form samples plus 5–10 micro-projects showing impact.

Use clear pricing tiers and explicit deliverables; include timelines and revision limits.

Write concise, benefit-focused proposals: state the problem, your approach, and the measurable outcome.

Collect social proof: client testimonials, ratings, and quantified results where possible.

Maintain a fast response time; many clients hire the fastest responsive freelancer.

Pricing guidance (ballpark)

Services with high perceived value (AI-assisted design, security reviews, automation): hourly $40–$150+ or project-based $500–$5,000+ depending on scope.

Development and analytics: hourly $50–$200+, with small projects around $1,000–$5,000.

Video and marketing: $200–$2,000+ per deliverable, scalable with complexity.
