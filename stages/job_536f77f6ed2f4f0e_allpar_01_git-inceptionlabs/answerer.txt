Key Takeaways
Traditional PoW mining still relies on hash functions, not on AI model computation.
“AI mining” generally refers to earning tokens by providing AI compute or services, not by solving cryptographic hashes.
Proof‑of‑Useful‑Work (PoUW) concepts that turn mining work into useful AI training exist in research labs, but none have become mainstream, widely‑adopted cryptocurrencies by 2026.
