Here’s a straightforward, step‑by‑step guide to making a classic homemade pizza from scratch.

Ingredients (for one medium pizza)
Dough
2 ½ cups all‑purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
1 cup warm water (≈110 °F / 43 °C)
2 tbsp olive oil
Sauce
1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
½ tsp salt
½ tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
Toppings (suggested)
1–2 cups shredded mozzarella
Pepperoni, sliced veggies, olives, mushrooms, etc. – whatever you like
Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
Instructions
1. Prepare the dough
Activate yeast: In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in the warm water. Let sit 5‑10 minutes until foamy.
Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together flour and salt.
Combine: Add the foamy yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Stir until a shaggy dough forms.
Knead: Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead 8‑10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
First rise: Lightly oil a clean bowl, place the dough inside, cover with a towel, and let rise 1‑2 hours, or until doubled in size.
2. Make the sauce
In a small saucepan, combine tomato sauce, minced garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar (if using).
Simmer on low heat 10‑15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Set aside to cool.
3. Assemble the pizza
Preheat oven to 475 °F (245 °C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it heats.
Shape dough: Punch down the risen dough, then roll or stretch it on a floured surface to your desired thickness (≈⅓‑½ inch). Transfer to a parchment‑lined baking sheet or a preheated stone.
Add sauce: Spread the sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust.
Add cheese & toppings: Sprinkle mozzarella, then arrange your chosen toppings.
4. Bake
Bake for 12‑15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
For extra browning, switch to broil for the last 1‑2 minutes (watch closely).
5. Serve
Let the pizza rest a couple of minutes, slice, and enjoy!
Quick Tips
Hot oven = crisp crust: Make sure the oven is fully pre‑heated.
Stone or inverted sheet: Improves bottom crust texture.
Don’t overload toppings: Too many can make the crust soggy.

Enjoy your homemade pizza! 🍕
