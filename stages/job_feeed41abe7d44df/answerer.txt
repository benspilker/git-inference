Basic Homemade Pizza (12-inch)
Dough (or use store-bought):
3½ cups (440g) bread flour, 1½ tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, 1 packet (2¼ tsp) instant yeast, 1¼ cups warm water, 2 tbsp olive oil
Mix dry ingredients, add wet, knead 8–10 min until smooth. Let rise 1–2 hrs until doubled.
Sauce:
1 can (14oz) crushed tomatoes, 1 garlic clove (minced), 1 tsp dried oregano, salt/pepper to taste. Simmer 10 min or use raw for fresher flavor.
Assembly:
Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with pizza stone or inverted baking sheet inside.
Punch down dough, stretch to 12-inch round on parchment.
Spread thin layer of sauce, add mozzarella (8oz), then toppings of choice.
Slide pizza (on parchment) onto hot stone/sheet.
Bake: 10–14 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles. Let rest 2 min before slicing.
Pro tips:
Don't overload toppings—less is more for crisp crust.
For crispier bottom: pre-bake crust 3 min before adding toppings.
No stone? Use a preheated cast-iron skillet or heavy baking sheet.
