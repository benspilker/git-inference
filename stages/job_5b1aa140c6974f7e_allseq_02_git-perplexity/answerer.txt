Here’s a simple homemade pizza method.

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.

1 tsp salt.

1 tsp sugar.

1 packet active dry yeast.

1 cup warm water.

2 tbsp olive oil.

1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes.

1 clove garlic, minced.

1 tsp dried oregano.

1–2 cups shredded mozzarella.

Your favorite toppings.

Dough

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

In a bowl, combine flour and salt.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.

Mix until a dough forms, then knead 8–10 minutes until smooth.

Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise 1–2 hours until doubled.

Sauce

Combine tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, and salt.

Simmer 10–15 minutes.

Let it cool slightly.

Assemble

Heat oven to 475°F.

Roll or stretch the dough into a pizza shape.

Put it on a baking sheet or hot pizza stone.

Spread sauce over the dough, leaving a small border.

Add cheese and toppings.

Bake

Bake 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve.

Easy tips

Preheat the oven fully for a better crust.

Don’t overload the pizza with toppings.

A pizza stone or upside-down baking sheet helps the bottom crisp up.
