Here’s a straightforward way to make a solid homemade pizza:

1) Dough (simple version)

2 ¼ tsp yeast (1 packet)

1 cup warm water (not hot)

2 ½ cups flour

1 tsp salt

1 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

Dissolve yeast in warm water (5–10 min until foamy).

Mix in flour, salt, and oil.

Knead ~8–10 minutes until smooth.

Let it rise in a covered bowl for 1–2 hours (until doubled).

(Shortcut: you can use store-bought dough.)

2) Sauce

1 cup canned crushed tomatoes

1 clove garlic (minced)

1 tsp olive oil

Salt + oregano/basil to taste

Mix and simmer ~10 minutes (or use it raw for a fresher taste).

3) Assemble

Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).

Roll/stretch dough into a circle.

Add a thin layer of sauce.

Sprinkle mozzarella cheese.

Add toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.).

4) Bake

Bake for 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.

5) Finish

Let it cool a couple minutes, slice, and eat.
