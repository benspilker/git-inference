Here's a concise, step-by-step guide to making a classic homemade pizza:
Dough (1 medium pizza)
2½ cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, 1 packet (2¼ tsp) active dry yeast
1 cup warm water (~110°F/43°C), 2 tbsp olive oil
Sauce
1 cup crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
1 clove garlic (minced), 1 tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp salt, pinch of sugar
Toppings
1–2 cups shredded mozzarella + your favorites (pepperoni, veggies, basil, etc.)
Steps
Activate yeast: Dissolve yeast + sugar in warm water; wait 5–10 min until foamy.
Make dough: Mix flour + salt, add yeast mixture + olive oil. Knead 8–10 min until smooth. Let rise in oiled bowl, covered, 1–2 hours until doubled.
Prep sauce: Simmer sauce ingredients 10–15 min on low heat.
Assemble: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out dough, spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
Bake: 12–15 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles. Optional: broil 1–2 min for extra browning.
Rest & serve: Cool 2–3 min before slicing.
💡 Pro tips
Preheat thoroughly—use a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet for crispier crust.
Don't overload toppings; excess moisture = soggy pizza.
For a faster version: use store-bought dough or a pre-made crust to skip the rise time.
