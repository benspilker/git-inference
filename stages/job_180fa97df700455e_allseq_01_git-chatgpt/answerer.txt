Here’s a clear, practical way to make a classic homemade pizza:

Ingredients (1 medium pizza)

Dough

2½ cups flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet (2¼ tsp) yeast

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

Sauce

1 cup tomato sauce

1 garlic clove (minced)

1 tsp oregano

Salt to taste

Toppings

1–2 cups mozzarella

Anything you like (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

1. Make the dough

Mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit 5–10 min until foamy.

Add flour, salt, and oil. Mix into dough.

Knead 8–10 min until smooth.

Cover and let rise 1–2 hours (until doubled).

2. Make the sauce

Simmer all sauce ingredients for ~10 minutes.

3. Assemble

Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).

Roll out dough.

Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.

4. Bake

Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.

5. Serve

Cool a few minutes, slice, and eat.

Quick tips

Hot oven = crisp crust

Don’t overload toppings

A preheated baking sheet or pizza stone helps a lot
