Here's a concise step-by-step guide to making pizza at home:

Prepare Dough: Combine 1 cup warm water, 1 teaspoon sugar, and 1 teaspoon yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes if using active dry yeast. Mix in 2 cups bread flour, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1 tablespoon olive oil. Knead for 6-8 minutes by hand or 4-6 minutes with a mixer. Let it rise for 60-90 minutes until doubled in size.

Shape the Dough: Once risen, divide the dough into two portions. Flatten into discs and let rest for 15-20 minutes.

Make Sauce: Combine 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 1-2 minced garlic cloves (or garlic powder), 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 teaspoon oregano, and ½ teaspoon basil. Add a pinch of sugar for sweetness, if desired.

Assemble Pizza: Stretch the dough, then top with sauce, cheese, and your preferred toppings.

Bake: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
