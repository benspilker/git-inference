Here’s a clear, practical guide to making a classic homemade pizza from scratch, plus a fast no-yeast option if you want it in ~15 minutes.

Classic Pizza (dough rises 1–2 hours)

Ingredients

Dough (1 medium pizza)

2½ cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet (2¼ tsp) active dry yeast

1 cup warm water (~110°F / 43°C)

2 tbsp olive oil

Sauce

1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes

1 clove garlic, minced

1 tsp dried oregano

½ tsp salt

½ tsp sugar (optional)

Toppings

1–2 cups shredded mozzarella

Pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, vegetables, etc.

Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

Activate yeast: Dissolve yeast + sugar in warm water; wait 5–10 min until foamy.

Make dough: Mix flour + salt; add yeast mixture + olive oil; form dough; knead 8–10 min until smooth and elastic.
joyfoodsunshine

Rise: Place in oiled bowl, cover, let rise 1–2 hours until doubled.
joyfoodsunshine

Sauce: Simmer tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, sugar 10–15 min.

Assemble:

Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C); use pizza stone if you have one.
