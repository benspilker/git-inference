How to Find Your First Customers (Step‑by‑Step)
Identify a micro‑niche – Pick a specific problem (e.g., “Instagram captions for boutique fashion stores”).
Create a one‑page landing page (no code needed – use Carrd or a simple HTML file) that clearly states the benefit, price, and a “Buy Now” button (PayPal/Stripe).
Leverage existing communities – Post a short, value‑first message in relevant subreddits, Discord channels, or Facebook groups. Offer a free trial or a discount for the first 5 buyers.
Show social proof quickly – After the first sale, ask for a testimonial or a screenshot and add it to the landing page.
Scale via marketplaces – List your prompt or micro‑content service on Fiverr/Upwork or a dedicated AI prompt marketplace. These platforms already bring traffic, and you only pay a commission after you earn.
Iterate based on feedback – Adjust pricing, add new prompt categories, or bundle services (e.g., “5 prompts + 1 custom copy”).
