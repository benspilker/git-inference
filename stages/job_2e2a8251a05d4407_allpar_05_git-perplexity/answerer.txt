Here’s a simple way to make a basic homemade pizza.

Dough

Mix:

2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet active dry yeast

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

Stir the yeast and sugar into the warm water. Let it sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

Mix flour and salt in a bowl.

Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, then stir until a dough forms.

Knead on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth.

Put the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise 1–2 hours until doubled.

Sauce

Mix:

1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes

1 minced garlic clove

1 tsp dried oregano

½ tsp salt

½ tsp sugar, optional

Simmer it for 10–15 minutes if you want a richer flavor.

Assemble

Heat your oven to 475°F.

Roll out the dough and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.

Spread sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.

Add mozzarella and your toppings.

Bake

Bake for 12–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling. Let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and serve.

Easy tips

Preheat the oven well for a better crust.

Use fewer toppings so the pizza stays crisp.

A pizza stone helps, but a baking sheet works too.
