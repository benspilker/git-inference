Here’s a clear, practical way to make a homemade pizza from scratch:

Ingredients

Dough (1 pizza):

2½ cups flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet (2¼ tsp) yeast

1 cup warm water

2 tbsp olive oil

Sauce:

1 cup tomato sauce

1 garlic clove (minced)

1 tsp oregano

Salt + pinch of sugar

Toppings:

Mozzarella cheese

Anything you like (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

1. Make the dough

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5–10 min until foamy.

Add flour, salt, and الزيت (olive oil).

Mix, then knead ~8–10 min until smooth.

Cover and let rise 1–2 hours (until doubled).

2. Make the sauce

Simmer all sauce ingredients for ~10–15 minutes.

3. Assemble

Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).

Roll out dough.

Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.

4. Bake

Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.

5. Cool & serve

Let sit a couple minutes, slice, and eat.

Quick tip: Hot oven + not overloading toppings = crisp, better pizza.
