Here’s a clear, practical way to make a homemade pizza from scratch:

Ingredients

Dough (1 pizza):

2½ cups flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

2¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast

1 cup warm water (~110°F)

2 tbsp olive oil

Sauce:

1 cup tomato sauce

1 garlic clove (minced)

1 tsp oregano

½ tsp salt

½ tsp sugar (optional)

Toppings:

1–2 cups mozzarella

Anything you like (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

1. Make the dough

Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5–10 min until foamy.

Combine flour + salt in a bowl.

Add yeast mixture + oil, mix into dough.

Knead 8–10 min until smooth.

Cover and let rise 1–2 hours (until doubled).

2. Make the sauce

Simmer all sauce ingredients 10–15 minutes on low.

3. Assemble

Preheat oven to 475°F.

Roll out dough.

Add sauce, cheese, and toppings.

4. Bake

Bake 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.

5. Serve

Let cool a few minutes, slice, eat.

Quick tips

Hot oven = better crust

Don’t overload toppings

Preheat your baking tray or stone if possible
