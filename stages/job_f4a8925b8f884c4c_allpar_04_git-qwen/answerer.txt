Here's a clear, step-by-step guide to making a classic homemade pizza from scratch:
Ingredients
Dough (1 medium pizza):
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar
1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
1 cup warm water (~110°F / 43°C)
2 tbsp olive oil
Sauce:
1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp sugar (optional)
Toppings:
1–2 cups shredded mozzarella
Pepperoni, vegetables, mushrooms, basil, or your favorites
Instructions
Make the dough: Dissolve yeast + sugar in warm water; wait 5–10 min until foamy. Mix flour + salt, add yeast mixture + olive oil. Knead 8–10 min until smooth. Let rise 1–2 hours in an oiled bowl.
Prepare sauce: Simmer tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar on low for 10–15 min.
Assemble: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out dough, spread sauce (leave a border), add cheese and toppings.
Bake: 12–15 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles. Optional: broil 1–2 min for extra browning.
Serve: Cool briefly, slice, enjoy.
💡 Pro tips: Preheat thoroughly for crisp crust; use a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet; don't overload toppings to avoid sogginess.
