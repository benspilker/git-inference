Here's a clear, step-by-step guide to making a classic homemade pizza from scratch:
Ingredients
Dough (1 medium pizza):
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar
1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
1 cup warm water (~110°F / 43°C)
2 tbsp olive oil
Sauce:
1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp sugar (optional)
Toppings:
1–2 cups shredded mozzarella
Pepperoni, vegetables, olives, mushrooms, or your favorites
Fresh basil for garnish (optional)
Instructions
Make the dough: Dissolve yeast + sugar in warm water; wait 5–10 min until foamy. Mix flour + salt, add yeast mixture + olive oil. Knead 8–10 min until smooth. Let rise in an oiled bowl, covered, 1–2 hours until doubled.
Prepare sauce: Simmer tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar on low for 10–15 min. Set aside.
Assemble: Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Punch down dough, roll out, and transfer to a baking sheet or preheated pizza stone. Spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
Bake: 12–15 min until crust is golden and cheese bubbles. Optional: broil 1–2 min for extra browning.
Serve: Rest 2–3 min, slice, and enjoy.
💡 Pro tips:
Preheat thoroughly—a hot oven = crispier crust.
Use a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet for better heat transfer.
Don't overload toppings; less is more to avoid sogginess.
