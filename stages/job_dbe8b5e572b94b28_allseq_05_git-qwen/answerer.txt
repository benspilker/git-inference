Homemade Pizza – Concise Step-by-Step
Dough (makes 2 medium pizzas)
Mix 3½ cups (440g) bread flour, 1¼ tsp instant yeast, 1¼ tsp salt, 1½ cups (360ml) warm water, and 1 tbsp olive oil.
Knead 5–7 mins until smooth. Let rise covered 1–2 hours (or refrigerate overnight for better flavor).
Sauce (quick version)
3. Blend 1 can (14oz/400g) crushed tomatoes with 1 minced garlic clove, 1 tsp olive oil, ½ tsp salt, and a pinch of dried oregano. No cooking needed.
Assembly & Bake
4. Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) with a baking steel/stone or inverted baking sheet inside.
5. Divide dough, stretch into 12-inch rounds on floured parchment.
6. Spread thin layer of sauce, add mozzarella and toppings sparingly (overloading = soggy).
7. Slide pizza (with parchment) onto hot surface. Bake 8–12 mins until crust is charred at edges and cheese bubbles.
8. Rest 2 mins, slice, finish with fresh basil or a drizzle of olive oil if desired.
Pro tips:
Use low-moisture mozzarella to avoid sogginess.
Pre-bake crust 2 mins if using wet toppings.
No stone? Use a preheated cast-iron skillet.
