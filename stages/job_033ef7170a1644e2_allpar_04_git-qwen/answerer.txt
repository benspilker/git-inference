Here's a clear, step-by-step guide to making a classic homemade pizza from scratch:
Ingredients
Dough (1 medium pizza):
2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
1 cup warm water (~110°F / 43°C)
2 tbsp olive oil
Sauce:
1 cup canned tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp dried oregano
½ tsp salt
½ tsp sugar (optional, balances acidity)
Toppings (suggested):
1–2 cups shredded mozzarella
Pepperoni, vegetables, olives, mushrooms, or your favorites
Fresh basil for garnish (optional)
Instructions
Make the dough:
Dissolve yeast + sugar in warm water; rest 5–10 min until foamy.
Mix flour + salt in a bowl; add yeast mixture + olive oil.
Knead 8–10 min until smooth. Let rise in an oiled bowl, covered, 1–2 hours.
Prepare the sauce:
Simmer tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar on low for 10–15 min.
Assemble:
Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Use a pizza stone if available.
Roll out dough, transfer to baking sheet or stone.
Spread sauce, add cheese and toppings (don't overload).
