Here’s a simple, reliable way to make pizza at home:

Ingredients

2½ cups flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 packet (2¼ tsp) yeast

¾ cup warm water

1 tbsp olive oil

½ cup pizza sauce

1–2 cups shredded mozzarella

Toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

Activate yeast
Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

Make dough
Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix and knead 8–10 minutes until smooth.

Let it rise
Cover and rest in a warm place for 1 hour (should double in size).

Preheat oven
Set to 475°F (245°C). Preheat a baking sheet or pizza stone if you have one.

Shape dough
Roll or stretch into a 10–12 inch circle.

Add toppings
Spread sauce, add cheese, then toppings.

Bake
Bake 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese bubbles.

Cool & serve
Let sit 2–3 minutes, slice, and eat.
