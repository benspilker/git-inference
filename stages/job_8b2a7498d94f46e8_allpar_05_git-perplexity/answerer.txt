Here’s the simplest way to make a basic pizza.

Dough

Mix together flour, yeast, salt, warm water, and a little olive oil.

Knead the dough for about 8 to 10 minutes until smooth.

Cover it and let it rise until doubled, usually about 1 to 2 hours.

Shape

Heat your oven as hot as it goes, ideally 475 to 500°F.

Punch down the dough and stretch it into a round on a floured surface.

Move it to a baking sheet, pizza stone, or pan.

Top

Spread on pizza sauce.

Add shredded mozzarella.

Add toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, or peppers.

Bake

Bake for about 10 to 15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Let it cool for a couple of minutes before slicing.

Easy formula

Dough: flour, yeast, salt, water, olive oil.

Sauce: crushed tomatoes, garlic, salt, oregano.

Cheese: mozzarella.

Toppings: whatever you like.

A quick beginner version is: buy store-bought dough, add sauce and cheese, then bake at high heat until the crust browns.
