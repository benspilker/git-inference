Bemenderfer LLC is a small, privately held software consulting firm based in Indianapolis, Indiana, founded in 2008 by David Bemenderfer. It specializes in Epicor Kinetic/ERP systems and offers consulting, implementation, training, integration, and process optimization services. The firm also develops a proprietary DataPortal product for mobile-friendly ERP data access and real-time integration.

Key details:

Employees: 2–10

Focus industries: Manufacturing, aerospace, food production, automotive, and other industrial sectors

Services: ERP consulting, project management, BAQs, dashboards, Crystal Reports/SSRS, CAD/BOM integration, automation, and custom API/connectors

Reputation: BBB accredited (A+ rating), emphasizes ethical, practical ERP solutions

Revenue/valuation (est.): ~$428K revenue, ~$1.4M valuation

Website: bemenderfer.com

The company emphasizes hands-on Epicor expertise, business process alignment, and long-term client relationships.
