Home‑Made Pizza – Concise Step‑by‑Step Recipe

Gather Ingredients

Dough: 2 ½ cups (300 g) all‑purpose flour, 1 tsp instant yeast, ¾ tsp salt, ½ tsp sugar, ¾ cup warm water (≈110 °F/43 °C), 1 Tbsp olive oil.
Sauce: ½ cup canned crushed tomatoes, 1 clove garlic (minced), ½ tsp dried oregano, ½ tsp dried basil, pinch of salt, 1 Tbsp olive oil.
Toppings: 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella, optional pepperoni, sliced veggies, fresh basil, etc.
Finishing: Extra‑virgin olive oil, grated Parmesan, red‑pepper flakes (optional).

Make the Dough

In a bowl, whisk flour, yeast, salt, and sugar.
Add warm water and olive oil; stir until a shaggy dough forms.
Knead on a lightly floured surface 6‑8 min (or use a stand‑mixer with a dough hook 5 min) until smooth and elastic.
Shape into a ball, coat lightly with oil, place in a bowl, cover, and let rise 1 h (or until doubled).

Prepare the Sauce

Heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in a small pan over medium heat.
Add minced garlic, sauté 30 sec.
Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, and salt; simmer 5‑7 min, stirring occasionally. Set aside to cool.

Preheat the Oven

Place a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet on the middle rack.
Preheat oven to 475 °F (245 °C) for at least 30 min (hot oven = crisp crust).

Shape the Crust

Punch down risen dough, divide into two equal pieces (for 12‑inch pizzas).
On parchment paper, stretch or roll one piece into a 12‑inch circle, leaving a slightly thicker edge for the crust.

Assemble the Pizza

Spread a thin layer of sauce (≈¼ cup) over the dough, leaving the edge clear.
Sprinkle mozzarella evenly.
Add desired toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.).
Drizzle a little olive oil over the top.

Bake

Slide the parchment‑paper‑on‑pizza onto the preheated stone/baking sheet.
Bake 10‑12 min, or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbling and lightly browned.

Finish & Serve

Remove pizza, let rest 2‑3 min.
Sprinkle fresh basil, Parmesan, and red‑pepper flakes if desired.
Slice, serve, and enjoy!

(Repeat steps 5‑7 for the second dough portion.)
